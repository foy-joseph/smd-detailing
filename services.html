FAQ

Things people usually ask before booking.

How far in advance do I need to book?

Currently around four weeks. School holidays and weekends fill first. Get on the list early — I'll confirm the exact slot once you send the form.

Can you come to me?

For now, only when a parent can drive me to you. That works fine for most jobs in Donegal — I just need a covered or sheltered space, a power socket and a tap. Once I'm on the road myself, I'll be fully mobile across Donegal and the border counties.

What if my car is much worse than average?

I'll give you an honest quote up front — sometimes the price moves to the upper end of a range, but I'll never spring a higher number on you at pickup. If the car genuinely needs a heavier scope, I'll say that before you book.

Is the Deluxe Detail really worth €400–600?

If you care about gloss, swirl marks and long-term protection — yes. The same job at a dealer or a high-street detailer would be €700–1,000+. The Deluxe is the package I built my international competition entry around.

What products do you use?

Professional detailing chemicals only — Koch-Chemie, Auto Finesse, Gtechniq-style sealants and ceramics, soft microfibre towels and proper grit-guard buckets. No supermarket spray.

Do you take cards?

Card, Revolut, bank transfer or cash. Whatever's easiest. Deposit goes when the slot is confirmed; balance on the day.

What's the story with North Macedonia?

My mini-company won 1st place in the All-Ireland competition and 6th in another, which qualified me for the international final in North Macedonia in October 2026. It's a real, run programme — and yes, the bookings keep coming while I'm there.
