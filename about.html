About

I'm 16. I'm from Donegal. And I might be Ireland's most obsessive young detailer.

My name's Shay McDevitt. SMD Detailing started as a Transition Year project. It's now booked a month ahead, won 1st place in an All-Ireland competition and is heading to the international final in North Macedonia.

How it started

I've been the kid washing the family cars since I was about ten. Slowly that turned from "give it a hose down" into "why is there a swirl mark on the bonnet and how do I get rid of it" — and from there into a serious interest in proper detailing: safe-wash methods, paint correction, ceramic coatings, leather care, the lot.

When Transition Year came around and the mini-company project was announced, the rest of my class was making candles and phone holders. I knew straight away what I was going to do. I'd already been quietly building up a kit out of pocket money — polishers, microfibres, chemicals — and I had a small list of family friends whose cars I'd cleaned. SMD Detailing was the obvious move.

The mini-company year

The mini-company programme is one of the biggest student-enterprise competitions in the country, and I went into it with one rule: every car had to leave better than any €40 valet anywhere in Donegal. No shortcuts. No "good enough for the price."

That stubbornness turned into bookings. Bookings turned into reviews. Reviews turned into a diary that was already a month out before the regional final. We won the regional. Then we placed 1st in the All-Ireland mini-company final, and 6th in another national competition — and qualified for the international final in North Macedonia in October 2026.

What North Macedonia means

It means I'm representing Ireland against young entrepreneurs from across Europe. It means everything I've built — the brand, the packages, the customer journey — gets stress-tested on an international stage. And it means when you book SMD Detailing, you're not booking "a young lad doing it on the side." You're booking someone whose work has already been judged at the highest level.

Why every car matters to me

This is what I want people to understand. Every single car that comes through SMD Detailing is done by me. There's no one else. So when I'm working on your car, it's not a job I'm getting through — it's my reputation sitting in your driveway. I take more care of your car than I do my own.

I'm also the right age to be obsessed with it. I don't have a mortgage to chase or staff to manage. I have time, patience, and a real love for the craft. That's what shows up in the finish.

What's next

Right now: keep delivering jobs my customers tell their friends about. After the Leaving Cert, I want SMD Detailing to grow into a proper Donegal detailing studio — fully mobile, fully booked, with a small reputation you can hear about across the county. North Macedonia is a chapter in that. So is your car.
